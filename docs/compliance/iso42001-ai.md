# ISO/IEC 42001:2023 — AI Management System (AIMS)

The world's first international standard for AI management systems. Published December 2023, it provides a framework for organizations to manage AI responsibly.

## Overview

ISO 42001 follows the same high-level structure (Annex SL) as ISO 27001, making integration straightforward for organizations already certified to ISO 27001.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Full title | AI Management System (AIMS) |
| Published | December 2023 |
| Structure | Annex SL (compatible with ISO 27001, 9001) |
| Scope | Organizations developing, providing, or using AI |
| Certification | Available (third-party audit) |

## Core Requirements (Clauses 4–10)

### Clause 4: Context of the Organization

- Understand internal/external factors affecting AI
- Identify interested parties (users, regulators, affected populations)
- Define the scope of the AI management system
- **AI impact assessment** — evaluate societal, ethical, and legal impacts

### Clause 5: Leadership

- Top management commitment to responsible AI
- AI policy that addresses:
  - Transparency and explainability
  - Fairness and non-discrimination
  - Safety and security
  - Privacy protection
  - Human oversight
  - Accountability

### Clause 6: Planning

- AI risk assessment methodology
- AI risk treatment plan
- AI objectives and planning to achieve them
- Address:
  - Bias and fairness risks
  - Safety and reliability risks
  - Privacy and data protection risks
  - Transparency and explainability needs

### Clause 7: Support

- Resources for AI management
- Competence and training requirements
- Awareness of AI policies
- Communication about AI practices
- Documented information management

### Clause 8: Operation

- Operational planning and control for AI systems
- AI system lifecycle management:
  - Data management (collection, quality, labeling)
  - Model development and validation
  - Deployment and monitoring
  - Retirement and decommissioning
- Third-party AI provider management

### Clause 9: Performance Evaluation

- Monitoring, measurement, and analysis of AI systems
- Internal audit of AI management system
- Management review

### Clause 10: Improvement

- Nonconformity and corrective action
- Continual improvement of AI management

## Integration with ISO 27001

Both standards use Annex SL, enabling integrated management:

| Shared Area | ISO 27001 | ISO 42001 |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| Risk management | Info security risks | AI-specific risks |
| Asset management | Information assets | AI systems + data |
| Access control | System access | AI model/data access |
| Supplier management | IT suppliers | AI providers |
| Incident management | Security incidents | AI incidents |
| Training | Security awareness | AI literacy |
